OpenCap

A lightweight, private, open-source screenshot tool. Only ~15 MB installed. No accounts, no cloud, no telemetry — just fast screenshots.

Download for Free View on GitHub
✂

Region Capture

Click and drag to select any area of your screen. Pixel-perfect selection every time.

📷

Full Screen Capture

Right-click to instantly grab your entire screen. No menus, no delay.

📋

Auto Clipboard

Screenshots are automatically copied to your clipboard and saved to your Pictures folder.

Open Source & Private

OpenCap is MIT licensed and 100% open source. Your screenshots never leave your computer — no cloud uploads, no analytics, no tracking. Every line of code is public and auditable.
